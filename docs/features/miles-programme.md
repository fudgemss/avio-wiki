# Miles Programme

Earn and redeem Avio Miles for tracked flights and bookings.

## Earning Miles

Miles are earned automatically for:

- Every flight booked through Avio
- Flights manually tracked and confirmed as flown
- Referrals (see below)

!!! info
    Miles are credited within 48 hours of a flight's completion.

## Checking Your Balance

/miles mymiles

## Redeeming Miles

| Redemption | Miles Required |
|------------|-----------------|
| $10 booking credit | 1,000 |
| $50 booking credit | 4,500 |
